About me

I am Pan Xinhao, ENTJ, resident in Hong Kong, currently studying LSGI major at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I like citywalking, hiking, visiting museums and exhibitions. I have been studying geography since college, and I am still working hard to obtain a doctorate. This major gave me the opportunity to see the beautiful scenery from all over the world, and gave me the motivation to use my camera to record this beautiful world. I am very glad that I can persist in photography in addition to scientific research. Although the shooting time is not long and the number of photos is not many, every time I press the shutter and take a beautiful photo, it gives me pure happiness. Regardless of whether the shot is good or not, it is the result of taking it seriously and trying hard. I believe that I will get better and better at shooting in the future. Finally, I hope to make more friends and record the world together. Only go to the mountains and seas to live up to your love!
